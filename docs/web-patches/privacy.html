Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Overview

MomentAI (“we”, “us”) turns photos you upload into mood-matched Spotify playlists. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It covers the MomentAI website and the MomentAI iOS / Android apps.

Information we collect

  • Account data — email, display name, and auth provider details when you sign in (via Supabase).
  • Photos you upload — images sent for mood analysis; we store them so you can reopen past moments.
  • Generation metadata — vibe labels, genres, audio features, and tracklists tied to your account.
  • Billing data (web) — on momentai.dev, payments are handled by Stripe; we store subscription tier and Stripe customer/subscription IDs, not full card numbers.
  • Billing data (iOS / Android apps) — in the native apps, subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store or Google Play (via RevenueCat). Apple/Google process payment details; we receive entitlement status (e.g. Plus active) and a store/app user identifier so we can unlock features. We do not receive your full card number.
  • Diagnostics & analytics — optional crash reports (e.g. Sentry) and product analytics (e.g. Google Analytics). These exclude photo contents, prompts, auth tokens, and email addresses when we control the payload.
  • Usage data — basic server logs (IP, timestamps, endpoints) for reliability and abuse prevention.

How we use information

  • Analyze uploaded photos (via Google Gemini) to recommend tracks.
  • Search and create playlists using the Spotify API.
  • Enforce free-tier daily limits and provide Plus billing.
  • Show your saved “Your Moments” history when signed in.
  • Improve reliability, security, and support.

Third-party services

We use Supabase (auth), Google Gemini (image analysis), Spotify (music catalog / playlist save), Stripe (web payments), Apple App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat (native subscriptions), and optionally S3-compatible storage for images. Their privacy policies apply to data they process on our behalf.

Retention

We keep account and generation records while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated moments in the app (You → Delete account), via delete-account.html, or by contacting us. Uploaded images and playlists may remain until deletion is completed.

Your choices

  • You can use the product without signing in for a limited anonymous generate flow on the web; history is not saved until you create an account.
  • Manage web billing in the Stripe customer portal; manage App Store / Play subscriptions in your device’s subscription settings.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.
